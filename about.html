About Campus Creators

Campus Creators is a multidisciplinary student club founded to bring together learners who enjoy creating — whether that's software, hardware, or campus events. Our members come from different branches and years, and we value collaboration and hands-on learning.

The club organizes workshops, collaborative projects, and speaker sessions. We focus on practical skills, teamwork, and building a portfolio that helps members when applying for internships and jobs.

Every semester we run a flagship hackathon and multiple mini-events where members can showcase their work and get feedback from peers and mentors.
